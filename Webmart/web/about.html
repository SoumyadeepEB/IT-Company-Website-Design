Approach Our Template Development

  1. Creative graphic artist visualizes and then proceeds to design the layout (5-10 hours)
  2. The template is then sent to our Quality Control team to inspect, if it fails to spark our interest, it is sent back for re-work. If it passes quality control, then it sent to next phase.
  3. Website designer cuts the Photoshop graphic file into HTML compatible pages (2-3 hours)
  4. The template is again sent to our Quality Control team who analyzes the HTML design quality, if it passes then it is available for download, otherwise it is sent back for adjustments

The average template takes about 7-15 hours to produce.
